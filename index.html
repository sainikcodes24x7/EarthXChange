    About

    As the issue of climate change becomes increasingly pressing, IEEE JUSB presents before all this World Environment Day EarthXChange, an event to promote environment sustainability and awareness. Powered by the enthusiasm of students and climate activists world wide, this event will provide a platform for the confluence of ingenious talent and academic expertise, thus making a humble contribution in our fight against climate change.
